Get to Know Hello Bixby

As Bixby gets familiar with how you use your phone, she will be able to display what you need, when you need it. When you access Hello Bixby, your cards will be displayed. You'll have cards for weather, news, scheduling, and many other things. Hello Bixby also helps you out by providing you a list of supported apps she thinks you'll like.

Available screens and settings may vary by wireless service provider and software version.

Setting Up Hello Bixby for the First Time

When you access Hello Bixby for the first time, you have to go through a few simple steps. Follow the guide below to breeze through it!

  1. To access Hello Bixby, press the Bixby key. You can also access Hello Bixby from the Home screen by swiping right.

  2. Touch START.

  3. Review and agree to Privacy Policy and Content Providers for Hello Bixby Features. To agree to both at the same time, choose I agree to all, and then touch FINISH.

  4. Hello Bixby is now set up. Swipe up on the screen to view more display cards.

Hello Bixby Cards

You can choose which cards to display on Hello Bixby. If you find some of the cards are not necessary you can disable them.

  1. To access Hello Bixby, press the Bixby key or swipe right on the Home screen.

  2. Touch Settings.

  3. To select which cards are displayed on Hello Bixby, touch Hello Bixby cards.

  4. Touch the slider next to the apps you want to display as cards.

    Cards that are disabled in HELLO BIXBY CARDS will not be available in HELLO BIXBY on Lock screen.

Download More Supported Apps

Interested in seeing more cards? Follow the steps below to find and download Hello Bixby supported apps just for you.

  1. To access Hello Bixby, press the Bixby key or swipe right on the Home screen.

  2. Touch Settings.

  3. Touch Supported apps.

  4. Touch DOWNLOAD next to the supported app you want to download.

  5. Touch INSTALL.
